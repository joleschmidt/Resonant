# Project Brief: Resonant

## Project Identity

**Name:** Resonant  
**Type:** German Guitar Marketplace (Kleinanzeigen Clone)  
**Domain:** Musical Instruments (Guitars, Amps, Pedals, Accessories)  
**Target Market:** Germany  
**Mission:** Provide German musicians with a scam-free alternative to generic classifieds platforms

## Core Purpose

Resonant is a specialized marketplace platform designed exclusively for:
- Guitars (electric, acoustic, bass, classical)
- Amplifiers
- Effects pedals
- Recording gear
- Guitar accessories (cases, strings, pickups, etc.)

**Primary Goal:** Enable German musicians to buy and sell used equipment without constant fear of fraud, phishing, or fake profiles.

## Key Differentiators

1. **Domain-Specific Focus:** Only guitar-related equipment (not generic classifieds)
2. **Trust & Safety First:** Comprehensive fraud detection, verification system, and anti-scam measures
3. **Modern Authentication:** Passkeys (WebAuthn) as primary method, MFA for sensitive actions
4. **Kleinanzeigen UX:** Familiar 1:1 UX matching eBay Kleinanzeigen patterns
5. **Thomann-Inspired Categories:** 3-level category hierarchy matching Thomann's structure

## Business Model

- Free basic accounts with limited listings
- Verified accounts for active sellers
- Premium/Store accounts for professional sellers
- Future: Transaction fees or premium features

## Success Criteria

1. **Trust:** Users feel safe transacting on the platform
2. **Usability:** Intuitive, familiar UX matching Kleinanzeigen expectations
3. **Quality:** Only legitimate guitar equipment listings
4. **Community:** Build a trusted community of German musicians
5. **Compliance:** Full DSGVO compliance with EU hosting

## Target Users

- **Sellers:** Musicians selling used equipment, small music stores
- **Buyers:** Musicians looking for quality used gear
- **Both:** Active community members who buy and sell regularly

## Constraints

- **Geographic:** Germany only (initially)
- **Language:** German UI and content
- **Category:** Guitar equipment only (strict enforcement)
- **Legal:** DSGVO compliance required
- **Hosting:** EU region (Frankfurt) for data residency

## Project Status

**Current Phase:** Post-MVP implementation  
**Last Major Update:** 2025-11-17 (System refactor with fraud detection)  
**Status:** Core features implemented, ready for testing and deployment
